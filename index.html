A Letter To Amma 💌

Dear Amma,

If sunsets could speak, they would speak gently like you. If stars could hug, they would feel warm like you. If love had a face, it would look exactly like yours.

Thank you for every sleepless night. Every sacrifice. Every silent prayer. Every time you worried without saying anything.

Thank you for feeding me before yourself. For smiling even when tired. For pretending to be strong even during difficult moments.

Your love never asked for attention. It simply existed quietly — beautifully — like moonlight on peaceful water.

Amma... I may grow older, but a part of me will always remain the child who runs toward you for comfort.

You are my first home. My forever home.

Happy Mother’s Day ❤️
