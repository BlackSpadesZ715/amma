A Letter To My Mother 💌

Dear Anoo,

If sunsets had a heartbeat, they would sound like your voice.

Soft. Warm. Comforting.

You are the calm after difficult days. The quiet strength behind every smile. The home hidden inside memories.

You stayed awake when I slept peacefully. You worried silently while pretending everything was okay. You carried burdens nobody noticed.

And still... you smiled.

You are not just my mother.

You are my safest place.
